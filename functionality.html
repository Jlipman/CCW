See All of the Cameras in Chicago


The city releases the locations of all active red light cameras and speed cameras in special safety zones. The app's main screen shows a map of all of these cameras and shows your location in relation to them. Now you can plan your route accordingly.


See The Cameras that are closest to you


The app will automatically calculate and show you the closest cameras, and their locations. Now you can discover the red light camera down the street that you never knew existed!


Be Warned of Approaching Cameras in Real Time


The app will warn you when you are about to approach a camera. It will send you a simple notification and say "approaching camera" out loud.


Customize the Warning to your Liking


The app includes the ability to customize the warning. You can customize the distance at which you are warned. After testing it, the creator of this app thought 405(ft) was a good distance. Though if you are fast driver, consider a larger distance. If you are a slower driver, consider a smaller distance.


It works with Speed Cameras too


This app also works with all of the special speed zones in the city, which contain speed cameras. You will get warned and alerted when you approach one of these also!
